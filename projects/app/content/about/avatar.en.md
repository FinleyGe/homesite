# About My Avatar

This is an oil painting created by Dutch-Belgian artist [Henriëtte Ronner-Knip](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henri%C3%ABtte_Ronner-Knip) (May 31, 1821 - February 28, 1909). The title is "Immodest".
